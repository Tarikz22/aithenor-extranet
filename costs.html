Housekeeping Labour — Systematic Overstaffing vs Forecast
Rooms Division · November 2025
CRITICAL
SITUATION

DIAGNOSIS
Scheduling is operating on a fixed headcount model, not a forecast-driven one. The schedule does not flex with occupancy. On 55% occupancy days the model shows 14 valets required — the gap widens to 12 excess valets on those days.
